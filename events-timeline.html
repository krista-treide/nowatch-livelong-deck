Why We Show Up — ROI & Goals
01
Pipeline & Deal Acceleration
Warm conversations compound — no ad replicates this. Prepare 20 high-priority meetings in advance and queue opportunities to move into a formal pipeline.
02
Brand & Field Feedback
Hear how our narrative lands — where it works and where it doesn't. Real objections and real desire, heard directly and unapologetically.
03
Credibility
Being in the room signals NOWATCH is serious and here to stay. All attendees are potential consumers — the B2C/B2B line blurs at events.
04
Partnerships
Prepare ideal corporate wellness partnerships, insurance providers, peer brands, thought leaders, credible clinicians and industry experts.
05
Evangelists & Community
Everyone at the event is a potential NOWATCH evangelist. Getting people of all levels to try on a NOWATCH in person builds belonging — and belonging spreads into inner circles, companies and beyond. Absence is also a signal — to clients, prospects, and talent.
06
Emotional Imprinting
The moment someone wears a NOWATCH and sees their own data in real time is a brand impression no ad can replicate. Felt experience converts faster than any funnel.
07
Earned Media
Excited attendees post, tag, and share on the spot.
08
Competitive Intelligence
Learn what Oura, Apple, WHOOP, and Garmin are saying in real time — booth messaging, who they're meeting, how they're positioning. Hard to get this any other way.
09
Data & Content Harvest
Every event generates raw material — testimonials, demo footage, stage moments, endorsements. One event feeds weeks of content and proof points post-show.
10
Board Visibility
These events specifically draw potential advisory members, policymakers and more. Being in these venues with a product is a strategic partnership move, not just sales.
